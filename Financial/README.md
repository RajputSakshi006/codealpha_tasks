# 💰 Financial Health Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The Financial Health Analytics Dashboard is designed to analyze the financial performance of small and medium-sized businesses (SMEs). It provides interactive visualizations to monitor revenue, expenses, profit, cash flow, and overall financial health, helping stakeholders make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze company revenue and expenses.
- Track profit and profit margin.
- Monitor monthly cash flow trends.
- Identify top-performing business categories.
- Forecast future financial performance.
- Support strategic financial planning.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

- Total Revenue
- Total Expenses
- Net Profit
- Profit Margin (%)
- Monthly Revenue Trend
- Monthly Expense Trend
- Cash Flow Analysis
- Category-wise Revenue
- Region-wise Performance
- Interactive Filters (Year, Quarter, Category, Region)

---

## 📂 Dataset

The dashboard is built using a financial dataset containing:

- Revenue

- Profit
- Cash Flow
- Business Category
- Region
- Date
- Sales Information
